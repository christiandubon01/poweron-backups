📋 Call Script Reference
GC / Relations Script (Improved)
OPENER
"Hi, this is Christian with Power On Solutions — we're a licensed electrical contractor in the Coachella Valley. Quick question: are you currently working with an electrical sub on your remodel or new construction projects?"
IF YES — they have an electrician
"Good to hear. I'm not looking to replace anyone — I work best as a backup when your guy is booked out or when a project needs a second crew. What does your typical electrical scope look like on a job?"
IF NO — use subs as needed
"Perfect. What type of work are you running right now — mostly remodels, new construction, commercial TI?"
IF in-house
"Understood. If you ever need overflow capacity or 24/7 emergency coverage, that's where we add the most value. Can I send you our license info and a quick overview?"
⚡ Rule: Qualify before you pitch. Short call, clear next step within 90 seconds.
Service / Property Manager Script (Improved)
OPENER
"Hi, this is Christian with Power On Solutions — licensed electrician serving the Coachella Valley. Do you handle maintenance decisions for your property, or is there someone else I should connect with?"
IF decision maker
"We specialize in service calls and electrical upgrades for commercial and residential properties. A lot of property managers we work with reach out when something isn't working — panels, GFCI issues, lighting problems. Is any of that on your radar right now?"
IF nothing active
"Makes sense. What's typically your go-to for electrical when something comes up — do you have a contractor on call?"
IF they have a contractor
"Good — a lot of properties keep us as a second call when their main guy isn't available or when they need someone same-day. Would it make sense to have our info on file for that?"
⚡ Rule: Free offer is last resort. Qualify decision maker in first 10 seconds.
⚡ 8-Step Lead Decision Framework
Step 1 — Classify
GC / Developer, Property Manager, Homeowner, or Service Call? Each gets a different track.
Step 2 — Advance Gate
Do they have budget? Is the job real? Do they have authority to hire? If no to any — Park.
Step 3 — Fast Disqualifiers
Too far (>45mi)? Scope outside license? Payment issues known? Kill immediately.
Step 4 — Response Buckets
ADVANCE (pursue), PARK (check back in 30d), KILL (log and close). Label every lead.
Step 5 — Calendar Fit
AM block ($420 target) or PM block ($260 target)? Does job timing fit current workload?
Step 6 — Go / No-Go
Does the estimated margin exceed your real cost/hr threshold? Below threshold = No-Go unless strategic.
Step 7 — Follow-up Cadence
GC: 2-week touchpoint. Service: same day callback. No response after 3 touches = Kill.
Step 8 — Weekly Source Review
Where did your Advance leads come from this week? Double down on top source. Cut low-yield sources.
